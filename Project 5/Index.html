Steve Jobs was the co-founder and CEO of Apple Inc. He is widely considered to be a brilliant businessman, as well as one of the greatest tech visionaries of our time.

The story of his life is nothing short of fascinating. After dropping out of college, he started Apple with his friend Steve Wozniakin 1976 at the age of 21. The duo gained wealth and fame a year later for the Apple II, one of the first highly successful massproduced personal computers. This was followed by the very successful Macintosh in 1984, the first mass-produced computer with GUI(graphical user intrface). Afetr being ousted of Apple in 1985, he went on to oversee the creation of two new companies: NeXt computer and Pixar. Witht eh success of the movie Toy Story, Steve became a billionaire.

In 1997, Syeve returned to Apple. Bill Gates and Microsoft dominated the computer industry, and Apple was on the brink of extinction, Steve was a fantastic salesma, and used hus business acumen to slowly bring the comapny back to profitability.
